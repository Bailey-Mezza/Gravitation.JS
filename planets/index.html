Space pauses or unpauses the simulation.

Arrow Keys / W A S D pan the camera (when paused).

1 zooms in (increases scale).

2 zooms out (decreases scale).

O toggles follow mode for a planet under the cursor (when paused).

I opens the planet editor for a planet under the cursor (when paused).

P opens the preset menu (when paused).

Left Click in the canvas (when paused) creates a new planet unless you click an existing one.

Click and Drag a planet to move it (when paused).
